About Stock Likes

Stock Likes is a stock market indicator based on social media. It is a web application that scrapes important social media metrics such as Likes and Retweets in order to generate a "buzz" factor. Based on the buzz factor and stock price, Stock Likes will indicate whether it is a good time to buy a stock.

Currently, I am working on development for the following companies:

  • Nvidia
  • Twitter

Once development of Stock Likes for these 2 companies has been perfected, I plan to release many more companies in time.

I hope you find this web application useful. Thanks for visiting!
